Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Iris: IPTV Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines our data practices.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, or phone number.

2. Device Identification

We use a unique, anonymized Device ID to manage your 7-day free trial and verify your lifetime Pro license status. This data is stored securely on our Supabase backend.

3. Content Privacy

We do not monitor, track, or store any IPTV playlists, M3U URLs, or specific content that you stream using our application. Your stream history remains local to your device.

4. Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat for payment processing and Supabase for license management. Both services comply with industry-standard security protocols.
